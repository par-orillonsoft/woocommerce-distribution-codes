=== WooCommerce Distribution Codes ===
Author: Tanner Linsley
Tags: woocommerce
Requires at least: 3.5
Requires WooCommerce at least: 2.0.0

Allows upload and distribution of codes with purchase of specific products

--- Installation ---

1. Upload the entire 'woocommerce-distribution-codes' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
